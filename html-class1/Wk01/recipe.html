Air Fryer Mozzarella Sticks

Indulge in the perfect blend of crispy and gooey with our Air Fryer Mozzarella Sticks. This healthier take on the classic appetizer delivers all the flavor without the guilt of deep frying. Stringy mozzarella is encased in a golden, herb-seasoned breadcrumb coating, air-fried to perfection. The result? A satisfyingly crunchy exterior that gives way to a molten cheese center. Serve these irresistible bites with your favorite marinara sauce for dipping, and watch them disappear at your next gathering. Quick to make and even quicker to vanish, these mozzarella sticks are sure to become your new go-to snack.

Ingredients:

  • 8 mozzarella string cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup Panko bread crumbs
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • salt & pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Cut mozzarella sticks in half.
  2. Whisk eggs with 1 tablespoon water.
  3. In a separate bowl combine bread crumbs, Panko bread crumbs, vegetable oil, and garlic powder.
  4. Place flour in a 3rd bowl and season with salt & pepper.
  5. Dip each mozzarella stick into the eggs and then into the flour mixture.
  6. Dip back into the eggs and finally into the bread crumb mixture.
  7. Place on a baking sheet and freeze at least 30 minutes (or up to 6 months).
  8. Preheat air fryer to 390 degrees.
  9. Generously spray mozzarella sticks with oil or cooking spray.
  10. Place in the air fryer basket in a single layer and cook

Fluffy Pancakes

Our secret lies in the perfect balance of ingredients and technique, resulting in pancakes that are light as air and melt in your mouth. Each golden disk boasts a tender, pillowy interior surrounded by slightly crisp edges. Whether you prefer them drizzled with warm maple syrup, topped with fresh berries, or smeared with creamy butter, these pancakes provide the ideal canvas for your favorite toppings. Easy to whip up on lazy weekend mornings or for a special brunch, these fluffy delights will have everyone at the table asking for seconds.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/3 cups milk
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil, or as needed for cooking
  • 3/4 tsp salt

Directions:

  1. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon and salt in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in the milk, egg, 3 tbsp vegetable oil and vanilla extract until only a few lumps remain.
  3. Let the batter stand for 5 full minutes for extra fluffiness.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add in a little vegetable oil.
  5. After the pan is preheated, slowly pour about 1/4 cup of batter into the hot pan.
  6. Cook until browned, about 2 minutes, then flip and cook until the pancake is set and the other side is browned about 2 more minutes.
